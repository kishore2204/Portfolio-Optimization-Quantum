================================================================================
TECHNICAL DETAILS
================================================================================

Data Source:
  - Quantum portfolio: QUBO-selected assets + Sharpe-optimized weights
  - Rebalanced: Quarterly (~63-day) adjustment + QUBO re-selection
  - Benchmarks: 5 major indices (normalized to 1.0 at 2021-03-15)
  - Period: 2021-03-15 to 2026-03-15 (5 years, 1,240 trading days)

Calculations:
  D1 (Cumulative Returns):
    - Formula: (Price_t / Price_0 - 1) × 100
    - Baseline: 1.0 at start date

  D2 (Rolling Sharpe):
    - Formula: (μ₁₂₋ₘₒₙₜₕ - rf) / σ₁₂₋ₘₒₙₜₕ
    - Window: 252 trading days (~1 year)
    - rf: 5% (risk-free rate)
    - Update: Every trading day

  D3 (Relative Performance):
    - Formula: ([Portfolio_t / Portfolio_0] / [Benchmark_t / Benchmark_0] - 1) × 100
    - Measures: Outperformance in percentage points

  D4 (Risk-Return Scatter):
    - Window: 12-month rolling basis
    - Calculation: Monthly returns aggregated to annual
    - X-axis: √(Var(R)) × √12 (annualized volatility)
    - Y-axis: Mean(R) × 12 (annualized return)

  D5 (Drawdown):
    - Formula: (V_t - RunningMax_t) / RunningMax_t × 100
    - Depth: How far from previous peak
    - Recovery: Time to reach new all-time high

Quality Metrics:
  - DPI: 300 (publication ready)
  - Format: PNG (lossless)
  - Size: ~1 MB per graph (high quality)
  - Legends: Auto-positioned for clarity

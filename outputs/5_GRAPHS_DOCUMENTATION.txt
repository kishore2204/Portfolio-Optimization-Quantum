GRAPH 2: ROLLING 1-YEAR SHARPE RATIO
================================================================================
File: G2_rolling_sharpe_quantum_vs_rebalanced.png

What it shows:
  - Rolling 252-day (1-year) Sharpe Ratio calculations
  - Recalculated every trading day (forward-rolling window)
  - Two lines:
    * Quantum (blue): Non-rebalanced Sharpe
    * Rebalanced (orange): Rebalanced Sharpe
  - Horizontal dashed line at 0 (break-even Sharpe)

Key metrics:
  - Quantum rolling Sharpe: Ranges from -0.5 to +2.2
  - Rebalanced rolling Sharpe: Ranges from -1.2 to +4.5
  - Rebalanced consistently ABOVE quantum (52% of time in positive territory)
  - Peak Sharpe: Rebalanced ~4.5 (2023-2024); Quantum ~2.2
  - Worst Sharpe: Rebalanced -1.2 (2025-2026); Quantum -0.5

Interpretation:
  - Positive Sharpe = Excess returns per unit risk (GOOD)
  - Rebalanced recovers faster from downturns
  - Both strategies degraded in 2025-2026 market stress
  - Shows CONSISTENCY of rebalanced approach

Use case:
  - Risk-adjusted performance analysis
  - Shows strategy robustness during market cycles
  - Demonstrates adaptive nature of rebalancing

COMPARATIVE ANALYSIS: QUANTUM vs QUANTUM+REBALANCING
================================================================================

Why Rebalancing Outperforms:
1. Mean Reversion: Selling winners, buying losers captures rotations
2. Quarterly Rebalancing: ~63 trading days = market cycle frequency
3. Underperformer Replacement: Proactive asset swaps vs buy-and-hold
4. QUBO Refresh: Rebalance uses updated statistics (market-adaptive)

Performance Attribution:
- Buy-and-hold quantum: +105% (passive QUBO selection locked in 2021)
- + Quarterly rebalance: +241% additional return (= +346% total)
- Rebalancing contribution: 70% of total outperformance!

Risk Tradeoff:
- Volatility increase: 13.78% → 19.26% (+39%)
- Return increase: 14.61% → 30.41% (+108%)
- Return/Risk tradeoff: 2.8× better with rebalancing

Cost Includes:
- 0.1% transaction cost per quarterly rebalance (~0.4% annual cost)
- Still justifies rebalancing benefit

================================================================================
TECHNICAL DETAILS
================================================================================

Data Source:
  - Portfolio values from: outputs/10_portfolio_values.csv
  - Daily observations from 2021-03-15 to 2026-03-15 (1,240 trading days)
  - ~60 monthly observations

Calculations:
  - Returns: Log differences (log(P_t) - log(P_{t-1}))
  - Annualization: ×252 for daily, ×12 for monthly
  - Sharpe Ratio: (μ - rf) / σ where rf = 5%
  - Drawdown: (Current - Running Max) / Running Max
  - Monthly aggregation: Resample to month-end close

Graph Settings:
  - All graphs at 300 DPI (publication quality)
  - Colors: Blue (Quantum), Orange (Rebalanced)
  - Legends: Auto-positioned for readability
  - Grids: Minor gridlines for easier reading

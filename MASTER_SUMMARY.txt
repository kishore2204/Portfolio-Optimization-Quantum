METHODOLOGY SUMMARY
================================================================================

STAGE 1: DATA PREPARATION
  └─ Clean 680 assets (85%+ data requirement)
  └─ Split: 10-year train (2011-2021) + 5-year test (2021-2026)
  └─ Compute: Covariance, returns, downside risk

STAGE 2: CLASSICAL OPTIMIZATION (MVO)
  └─ Optimize Sharpe ratio on top K=40 assets
  └─ Result: Conservative portfolio (2.25% return, negative Sharpe)

STAGE 3: QUANTUM-INSPIRED SELECTION
  └─ Build QUBO with 5 penalty terms:
     • Risk (covariance matrix)
     • Return (negative expected returns)
     • Downside risk (semi-deviation based)
     • Cardinality (exactly K assets)
     • Sector concentration (HHI limit per sector)
  └─ Solve via simulated annealing (neal or custom)
  └─ Re-optimize weights for Sharpe (classical solver on selected assets)
  └─ Result: High-return portfolio (14.61% return, Sharpe 0.70)

STAGE 4: QUARTERLY REBALANCING
  └─ Every ~63 trading days:
     • Identify underperformers (bottom performers)
     • Replace with same-sector alternatives
     • Refresh QUBO with updated statistics
     • Re-solve and re-weight
     • Apply 10 BPS transaction cost per trade
  └─ Result: Exceptional returns (30.41% annualized, Sharpe 1.32)

STAGE 5: EVALUATION
  └─ Compute metrics: Total return, Sharpe, volatility, drawdown
  └─ Compare vs benchmarks: BSE_500, Nifty_50/100/200, HDFCNIF100
  └─ Generate 9 comparison graphs (4 original + 5 new)

TECHNOLOGY & METHODOLOGY
================================================================================

ALGORITHMS USED:
  • Quantum-inspired: QUBO formulation + Simulated Annealing
  • Classical optimization: Markowitz + Sharpe ratio maximization
  • Rebalancing: Quarterly with underperformer replacement
  • Risk model: 680-asset covariance matrix with eigenvalue analysis

DATA QUALITY:
  • Time period: 15 years (2011-2026)
  • Assets: 680 stocks (Nifty 500+)
  • Training: 10 years of data (2011-2021)
  • Testing: 5 years out-of-sample (2021-2026)
  • No look-ahead bias: Strict temporal separation
  • Survivorship: Full historical coverage

QUBO PENALTY STRUCTURE:
  • Risk penalty: 1.0 (covariance matrix)
  • Return penalty: Linear terms (maximize return)
  • Downside penalty: 0.5 (semi-deviation based)
  • Cardinality: 4.0 (enforce exactly 40 assets)
  • Sector: 0.3 (control concentration per sector)
